Majors

Business Administration

Degrees Awarded (2023): 276

The General Business Administration & Management major is the most common bachelor's degree concentration at TCNJ. This program provides a comprehensive foundation in business principles, preparing graduates for various roles in the corporate world.

Biological Sciences

Degrees Awarded (2023): 137

TCNJ's Biological Sciences program is highly competitive, with over 1,200 applications for approximately 100 spots. This major offers tracks in general biology, secondary education, and dual-degree pre-professional programs, including pathways to medicine, optometry, pharmacy, and physical therapy.

Psychology

Degrees Awarded (2023): 134

The Psychology major at TCNJ provides students with a deep understanding of human behavior and mental processes. Graduates often pursue careers in counseling, social services, or continue their education in graduate programs.
