Sports

Varsity Athletics

TCNJ is an NCAA Division III school with over 20 varsity teams competing in the New Jersey Athletic Conference (NJAC). The Lions are nationally recognized for excellence, especially in sports like track & field, lacrosse, and field hockey.

Club Sports

Students can join competitive club teams such as rugby, ultimate frisbee, ice hockey, and volleyball. Club sports offer a balance between competition and fun, with opportunities for travel and tournaments.

Facilities

TCNJ students enjoy top-notch facilities, including the DeCou Field Complex, Packer Hall, and the Aquatic Center. The Rec Center is open daily for workouts, pickup games, and group fitness classes.

TCNJ Complex

TCNJ Baseball Field

The TCNJ Baseball Field is a state-of-the-art facility where varsity and intramural baseball teams practice and compete. Fans can enjoy games from comfortable seating areas, and the field is maintained to NCAA standards. Special events and summer camps are also held here, making it a hub for student and community activity.
